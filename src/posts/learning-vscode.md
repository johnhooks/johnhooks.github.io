## Learning VSCode

### Keyboard Shortcuts

- `[Alt]+[Tab]` - `workbench.action.quickSwitchWindow`
- `[Cmd]+[&#96;]` - toggle between open windows
- `[Cmd]+[K] -> [S]` - `File: Save without Formatting`
